    How It Works

    Welcome to a living, collaborative story that never ends—built one word at a time by everyone. It’s fast, fair, and fully autonomous.

    • Submit & Style: Type a word and use the buttons to make it bold, italic, underlined, or start a new line (↵) before submitting.
    • Vote: Use ▲ / ▼ to vote on words in the live feed. You can’t vote on your own submissions. The winner is the highest net score (upvotes minus downvotes).
    • Win the Round: Every 60 seconds the round ends. The word with the highest score (must be > 0) is permanently added to the story. It will be automatically capitalized if it begins a new sentence.
    • Seal & Archive: Four times a day, the story is sealed into a permanent “chunk” with a unique cryptographic hash so its history can’t be altered.
    • Automatic Images: On each seal, an AI illustration is automatically generated to match the newly sealed chunk. A placeholder appears while it renders; the image swaps in as soon as it’s ready.

    The Bot: To keep things moving, an AI helper (sntnz_bot) will propose a word if the feed has been quiet for 3 minutes. Its words can be voted on like any others.

    Fully Independent: No manual steps. The system runs the rounds, seals the story, generates the art, and posts to social networks automatically at every seal.

    Why it’s cool: It’s a new kind of digital literature—part game, part collective improv, part autonomous art machine. Jump in, shape the narrative, and watch your choices get sealed into history.

    What’s next: Broader broadcasting and a wallet-based system to give contributors true ownership of the parts they helped create.
