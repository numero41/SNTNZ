    A Story That Writes Itself

    This is a living narrative powered by an autonomous system that generates its own story, art, and social media posts. It's an endless art experiment where you can contribute to and influence the final creation.

    The Rhythm of Creation

    • The Word Round (Every 60 Seconds): Users submit and vote on words in a live feed. The highest-scoring word is permanently added to the story.
    • The Chapter Seal (Four Times a Day): The story is automatically archived into a permanent "chunk," secured with a cryptographic hash, and given a unique AI-generated illustration.

    The Autonomous Core

    • AI Storyteller: The sntnz_bot initiates new sagas by writing complete opening chapters or concludes stories started by the community.
    • AI Illustrator: For each sealed chunk, an AI artist interprets the text and creates a unique illustration to match it.
    • Automatic Broadcaster: At every seal, the system automatically posts the new text and its illustration to social networks.

    Explore the Living Archive

    • Interactive Text: Click any word to see its author, timestamp, and the vote data that made it a winner.
    • Highlight Contributors: Filter the story to highlight all contributions from a specific author.
    • Journey Through History: Use the history pages to read the full story from any day since its inception.
    • Share a Moment: Every sealed chunk has a unique URL. Use the Share button to post a link to any moment in the story.

    What's Next: True Ownership

    The future involves integrating blockchain technology. Each chunk will be sealed on-chain, creating a provably permanent and censorship-resistant literary artifact. This will be paired with a wallet-based system to give contributors true, verifiable ownership of the parts of the story they helped create.
