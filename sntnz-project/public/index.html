    How It Works

    This is a collaborative writing experiment to create a story that never ends, built one word at a time by everyone.

    • Submit & Style: Type a word and use the buttons to make it bold, italic, underlined, or start a new line (↵) before submitting.
    • Vote: Use the up (▲) and down (▼) arrows to vote for words in the live feed. You cannot vote on your own submissions. The winner is determined by the highest net score (upvotes minus downvotes).
    • Win: Every 60 seconds, the round ends. The word with the highest score (must be greater than zero) is permanently added to the story. It will be automatically capitalized if it begins a new sentence.
    • Archive: Every 250 characters, the story is sealed into a permanent "chunk" with a unique cryptographic hash, ensuring its history can never be altered.

    The Bot: To keep the narrative flowing, an AI-powered bot (sntnz_bot) will contribute a word if the live feed has been empty for 3 minutes. Its suggestions can be voted on just like any other submission.

    The Future: The vision is to grow this into a new form of digital literature. Future plans include broadcasting the story to social networks and implementing a system using blockchain wallets to give contributors true ownership of the parts of the story they helped create.
