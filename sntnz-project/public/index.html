    How It Works

    This is a collaborative writing experiment to create a story that never ends, built one word at a time by everyone.

    • Submit & Style: Type a word in the input field. You can use the buttons to make it bold, italic, or underlined before submitting.
    • Vote: You can vote for any submitted word in the live feed simply by clicking on it. The more clicks a word gets, the higher its vote count.
    • Win: Every 60 seconds, the round ends, and the word with the most votes is permanently added to the story.
    • Archive: Every 250 characters, the story is sealed into a permanent "chunk" with a unique cryptographic hash, ensuring its history can never be altered.

    The Bot: To keep the narrative flowing, an AI-powered bot (sntnz_bot) will contribute a word if no one else has made a suggestion during a round. Its suggestions appear in the live feed and can be voted on just like any other submission.

    The Future: The vision is to grow this into a new form of digital literature. Future plans include broadcasting the story to social networks and implementing a system using user accounts or blockchain wallets to give contributors true ownership of the parts of the story they helped create.
